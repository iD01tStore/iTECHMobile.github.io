1. Information we collect

We collect the minimum information needed to diagnose and repair your device and to operate our platform.

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Contact information ‚Äì such as your name, email address, and optionally your phone number or billing address.
  • Account / technician details ‚Äì when you apply as a technician, we may collect information about your repair experience and business.

1.2 Device & diagnostic information

When you run the iTECHMobile USB Client and connect your Android device, the client may collect:

  • Basic device identifiers (model, manufacturer, OS version, build information).
  • Technical status data needed for diagnostics (boot state, available storage, error codes, logs where applicable).

We do not deliberately copy the personal content of your device (such as your photos, messages, or personal documents) as part of standard repairs. However, technicians may see your screen during live sessions, similar to handing your phone to a physical repair shop.

  • High-level, anonymized statistics about repair success rates and common issues.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the iTECHMobile USB Client and Technician Dashboard.
  • To diagnose issues on your device and estimate repair success probability and pricing.
  • To route your repair session to an available, whitelisted technician.
  • To process payments and deliver invoices or receipts.
  • To improve our AI diagnostics and repair procedures, using aggregated and anonymized data when possible.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To maintain security, prevent fraud, abuse, and misuse of the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With technicians: device and diagnostic information, and relevant contact details, so they can perform your repair and communicate about the job.
  • With service providers: trusted third parties that help us operate the platform (for example: hosting, analytics, logging, tunnel providers, and payment processors).
  • For legal reasons: if required to comply with laws, regulations, legal processes, or enforceable governmental requests, or to protect our rights, users, and systems.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to continuity of privacy protections.
