The Power of AI Diagnostics

Success Probability

Our model analyzes device logs, fault patterns, and known fix paths to provide a success likelihood before you pay, minimizing your risk.

Complexity & Risk

The AI assigns a complexity score (Simple, Medium, Complex). This score directly determines the fixed price and the level of certified technician required.

Transparent & Fixed

All pricing is fixed and dynamic, determined by the AI's risk assessment. No hourly rates, no surprise fees. Whether it’s a boot loop, FRP lock, MDM profile, or OS corruption, you pay the quoted price or may qualify for a refund according to our policy.

Security, Speed, and Reliability

Unrivaled Remote Security

  • End-to-End Encrypted Tunnel

    Repair is conducted over a temporary, secure ngrok tunnel. No public ports or exposed connections.

  • Visual-Only Control via scrcpy

    Technicians are restricted to screen mirroring (scrcpy) and a small, audited set of ADB commands required for the repair. They do not receive unrestricted shell access or direct file-system / cloud account access.

  • Certified, Whitelisted Technicians

    Only vetted and certified repair professionals gain access to the secure job queue and dashboard.
