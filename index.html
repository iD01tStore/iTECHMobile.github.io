Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from customers and technicians.

What devices do you support?
We currently focus on Android smartphones and tablets. Support may vary by manufacturer, chipset, and Android version. The iTECHMobile Client will automatically check compatibility during the diagnostic step.
Do you see my personal data?
Technicians see your screen during the repair session, similar to handing your device to a local shop. We only access settings and tools required for the repair and do not intentionally browse your personal content. You can disconnect at any time if you're uncomfortable.
What if the repair fails?
If our AI engine estimates a low probability of success, we will tell you before you pay. In cases where a repair cannot be completed after payment, our support team reviews the session for a partial or full refund based on our refund policy.
Is this safe for my device?
Any repair carries some risk, but our AI includes a risk assessment and our technicians follow standardized procedures. High-risk operations are clearly indicated and may be declined if the chance of permanent damage is too high.
Do I need to stay at the computer during the repair?
We recommend staying nearby while your device is connected. You can watch the entire repair via live mirroring and disconnect once we are done.
How do I become a technician on the platform?
Experienced Android repair professionals can apply by emailing itechinfomtl@gmail.com. We'll ask for your experience, verify your skills, and whitelist your dashboard login if approved.
